Facebook

What is Facebook?

Facebook is a social networking website that allows people to connect with friends, family and communities around the world. Users can share photos, videos, messages and updates about their daily life.

How to Use Facebook?

Users can create a personal profile and add friends. After adding friends they can post updates, share photos, send messages and join groups. People can also like and comment on posts shared by others.

How to Create a Facebook Account?

  1. Go to the Facebook Website
  2. Click on Sign Up
  3. Enter your name, email or phone number and password.
  4. Select your date of birth and gender.
  5. Click Create Account.
  6. Verify your email or phone number.

History

Facebook was created in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his friends at Harvard University. The website was first developed for college students. Within a short time it became popular among universities and later opened for the public. Today Facebook is one of the biggest social networking platforms in the world.

Journey

  • 2004 - Facebook launched at Harvard.
  • 2005 - Expanded to other universities.
  • 2006 - Opened for everyone above 13 years.
  • 2012 - Reached 1 billion users.
  • 2021 - Company name changed to Meta.

Main and Important Features

  • User Profiles
  • Friend Requests
  • News Feed
  • Photo and Video Sharing
  • Messenger Chat
  • Pages and Groups

Meta Technologies

Artificial Intelligence

Meta develops advanced AI systems for recommendations and content moderation.

Machine Learning

Machine learning improves news feed ranking and advertising systems.

Large Language Models

Meta created LLaMA models used in AI assistants and chatbots.

React

A JavaScript library used to build modern user interfaces.

PyTorch

A deep learning framework widely used in artificial intelligence research.

GraphQL

A modern API query language used to efficiently request data.

Computer Vision

Used for photo recognition and automatic tagging in images.

Virtual Reality

Meta develops VR systems like Meta Quest for immersive digital worlds.

Data Centers

Large global infrastructure that powers Facebook services.

Multimodal AI

AI systems capable of understanding text, images and videos together.
